What is ReusedTech?

ReusedTech is a startup online retailer, we sell phones, computers, consoles and accessories for other devices. We are based in Dublin Ireland, but we can serve customers from all of europe, and in some locations in asia. the main goal of this start up is to reduce the negative impact of phone production on the environment. Buying a used phone is both cost efective and good for the environment, it is a great way for someone who upgrades their electronic devices regularly to reduce their carbon footprint.
