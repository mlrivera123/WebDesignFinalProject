Who are we

ReusedTech was founded in Dublin in 2022, We are a group of students tasked to make a website consisting of over 10 web pages, for our projects Topic we chose to make a Website on an online store that sells Refurbished and second hand Technology such as Computers, Phones, gaming consoles and other accessories, we took inspiration from a leading online refurbished seller Swappie. Swappie sells refurbished iphones and accessories, we decided to also incorporate computers and gaming consoles into our website to make it appeal to everyone

The impact of e-waste on the Environment

e-waste is not biodegradable and accumulates in the enviroment over time, in the soil, water, and living things. According to the international waste electrical and electronic equipment (WEEE) forum, aproximately 5.3 billion mobile phones alone will be thrown out this year. This is very concerning as these phones let alone all the other electronic devices that will be thrown our, will release toxic material in the world, impacting the environment and the living organisms around them.
